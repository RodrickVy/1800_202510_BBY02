About Us

Welcome to RECREA-8, a platform dedicated to connecting players, teams, and captains in recreational volleyball leagues. We make it easy for players to find substitutes and captains to fill in spots quickly, ensuring that every game is played smoothly.

Our Vision

We aim to create a welcoming volleyball community where players of all skill levels can connect, improve, and play. Our platform ensures captains can find substitutes effortlessly, while players can easily step into games when needed.

Our Mission

At RECREA-8, we are committed to simplifying the substitution process for recreational volleyball leagues, providing players and captains with a quick and easy way to find each other, making sure every game has the right players at the right time.

Our Team

Our team is passionate about both volleyball and technology, working to improve our platform based on community feedback to help captains and players stay connected, no matter the circumstances.
